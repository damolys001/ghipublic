Welcome to GHI Assets

GHI Assets Ltd was incorporated under the laws of Nigeria in 2004. It is an investment company holding varying interests in the different companies within the group. We specialize in airline representation and management, business and leisure travel management,training and travel technology solutions.
GHI Assets has business interests in Angola, Benin Republic, Cameroon, Gabon and Nigeria with plans to expand into more African countries.
We strive to be market leaders in our businesses employing cutting edge technology to achieve sustainable profitable growth.

Tour Brokers International Nigeria

Tour Brokers International Limited (TBI) was incorporated in 2002 to professionally package tourism services to selective destinations for Nigerian business and leisure travelers and to expatriates living in Nigeria. TBI’s goal is not only to meet the discerning travel needs of its clients but also to anticipate them well in advance. TBI offers tailor-made tour packages to suit the varying requirements of individuals, families, groups and corporate bodies.

TBI’s strategic partnership with international companies such as Royal Caribbean International and GTA, combined with an array of unrivaled innovative ideas and high credibility on delivering on her promise gives it a strong competitive advantage over others in the market. With a staff strength of 38, TBI’s highly trained and experienced Travel consultants ensure that all clients get optimum value for money.

Business Travel Management Limited

Travel Management Limited (BTM) , is a member of the GHI Assets Ltd group. Business Travel Management Limited was incorporated in 2004 and is an ISO 9001-2008 accredited agent as well as being accredited by I.A.T.A. (International Air Transport Association).

In July 1999, the company which specializes in comprehensive travel management, corporate services and logistics for businesses, became the top provider of corporate travel management, B2B logistic solutions, and the pacesetter in the application of technology and innovative travel management in Nigeria. Business Travel Management Limited quickly expanded to service clients throughout West & Central Africa.

The group acquired Transcap Travel Bureaux Limited (TTBL) in 2007. The company remains one of the leading travel management companies in Nigeria with a long-standing track record of successes since its days as a travel division within the French Multinational CFAO for almost six decades.

SKY LOGISTICS LIMITED
