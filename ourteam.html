Our Team

Olufemi Adefope

CHAIRMAN, GHI Assets Ltd

Olufemi Adefope has over 40 years experience in the aviation, travel management and corporate services industry. He is currently Chairman of GHI Assets Ltd which holds various interests in companies representing Airlines, Travel and Tours in Nigeria as well as in other African countries.

Some of these include

  • Business Travel Management Ltd
  • Sky Logistics Ltd (the General Sales Agent for Delta Air Lines Inc.)
  • Sky Logistics Cargo Ltd
  • Total Air Logistics Ltd, (the General Sales Agent for Kenya Airways)
  • Tour Brokers International Ltd (the leading tour operator in Nigeria specialising in outbound tour packages)
  • Transcap Travel Bureaux Ltd
  • Frontier Travel Academy (which offers IATA/UFTAA certified air transportation courses)
amongst others

He introduced South African Airways to Nigeria in 1997 and ran the General Sales Agent for ten years, trading as Airline Management Ltd. Olufemi Adefope attended Howard University and George Washington University both in the USA, where he undertook a BSc in Zoology and MSc in Forensic Science respectively. He is an alumni of the Lagos Business School (Chief Executive Programme 15).

Mrs Adebola Adefope

Director

Mrs Adefope is a legal practitioner. She holds a Masters degree in Law (LLM) from London School of Economics, University of London.

She was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1980 and has practised both in Nigeria and U.K.

She was enrolled as a Solicitor of the Supreme Court of England and Wales & Election as a member of the Law Society 1997

She has a wealth of legal and business experience that spans over 35 years and has been on the Board of several Companies.

She is now Group Counsel to GHI Assets Limited. She is on the Board of several companies.
